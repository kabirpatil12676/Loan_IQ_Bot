// Capabilities

Built for Real Insights

Not just a score — LoanIQ explains why and tells you how to improve.

🧠
ML-Inspired Scoring Model
Built on the same principles as the Loan Approval Prediction ML project — a weighted multi-factor model that mirrors how banks evaluate applicants. Factors like DTI ratio, credit score tiers, and income-to-loan multiples are benchmarked against real lending data. The bot doesn't just give you a number — it surfaces the exact factors dragging your score down.
⚠️
Risk Warnings
Flags specific concerns like high debt ratio, low credit score, or loan amount exceeding income limits — with targeted improvement tips.
📐
Live EMI Calculator
Calculates your estimated monthly EMI and debt-to-income ratio in real time using the standard amortisation formula.
🔄
Re-check Anytime
Type "Check Again" to restart with different values — useful for exploring how improving your credit score or reducing loan amount changes your result.
🌐
Conversational Interface
Powered by Google Dialogflow ES with quick-reply buttons, context-aware prompts, and graceful fallbacks for invalid inputs.
